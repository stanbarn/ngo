Good mental health starts in childhood.

The NewLife Foundation strives to provide basic medical services and to keep the population of our communities healthy and free from treatable diseases. Our physicians travel to different communities each half-day to meet with members of the communities and perform medical consultations at one of our Rainbow Network medical clinics. These physicians are equipped with a variety of medications to treat basic health problems. While NewLife Foundation purchases these medications, large quantities of normal and prenatal vitamins, as well as anti-parasite medications, are generously donated by organizations here and abroad.

Not only does NewLife Foundation take responsibility in providing basic healthcare, we also arrange for any follow-up appointments or special exams, should they be necessary. In circumstances that surpass basic healthcare, we assign patients to our Special Medical Needs program.

We aspire to be a welcoming community, sharing our passion and energy to improve the lives of others. Some of our core values include.

  • Enjoy growing up and are more able to deal with difficult times
  • Develop positive relationships with friends and family
  • Develop their understanding of right and wrong
  • Enjoy learning and start doing better at school
  • Have a positive identity and self-worth
  • Enjoy new experiences

NewLife Foundation staff is available to provide education and training for individuals who want to open and run a small business of their own. Our staff meets with members of the community to discuss the different aspects of business and economies, and works with individuals to help them get started.
