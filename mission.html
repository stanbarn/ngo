Our Mission

Our Mission

  • Caring: A deep commitment to helping others, a dedication to helping each other to give more and better;
  • Taking action: Following through with our commitments, acting based on evidence, researching new opportunities, putting our money where our mouth is;
  • Openness: Being open-minded towards new evidence and reasoning, honest about what we don’t know, open about mistakes we’ve made;
  • Optimism: An ambitious and enthusiastic belief that we can create significant positive change and build a better world.
Education is the foundation for a stronger future for all.

All students receive new school supplies each year, including backpacks, notebooks, pencils, markers, glue, etc. All students attend Rainbow Network Nutrition Centers, where they receive a nutritious meal and may participate in local cooking or baking classes. Students also receive frequent medical check-ups and education on disease prevention and proper hygiene.

Our education program includes both primary and secondary students as they learn, play, and grow. We currently planning to operate 8 primary schools with over 3,000 students across rural Uganda. We have also partnered with approximately 4 high school students in our scholarship program.
